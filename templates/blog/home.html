Quality Suites

As data quality becomes an increasing consideration for companies investing in market research, it’s important to verify respondent authenticity. Question Quest Quality Suites is a comprehensive validation approach to ensure the quality of studies.

IP Verification

  • IP Address of new panelist validates location, in order to ensure they are within the market region.
  • Checked against a Known list of fraudulent servers to ID fraud at the time of recruitment

Duplication Prevention

  • It’s also important to prevent the same “person” from completing the same survey more than once, either because they are coming from multiple panels being used on a project or exist multiple times within the same panel.

Loyalty Detector

  • Fraud respondents blocked from entering surveys.
  • Proven effective regardless of sources market or audience type.
  • Offered standard on all consumer work over 10% IR

Unique Survey Responders

  • Eliminates duplicates at the start of a study.
